How it works

Three quick steps

  1. 1

    Sort into three piles

    First glance at each card and drop it into Agree, Neutral, or Disagree. Quick gut feeling.

  2. 2

    Build the pyramid

    Place every card into the grid from −5 (strongly disagree) to +5 (strongly agree). Each column holds a fixed number of cards, so you'll compare and decide what matters most.

  3. 2

    Rate each statement

    Give every statement a score from −5 to +5. No limits on how many can share a score.

  4. 3

    Tell us why

    A few short questions about your strongest choices, and you're done.
